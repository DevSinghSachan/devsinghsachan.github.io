Devendra Singh Sachan

I am a PhD student at Mila and School of Computer Science, McGill University, where I am fortunate to be advised by William Hamilton. My research interests are in natural language processing and machine learning.

I completed my masters from the Language Technologies Institute, School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University, where I was lucky to work with Graham Neubig, Ruslan Salakhutdinov, and Eric Xing. Previously, I did my bachelors at Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati, where I was advised by Amit Sethi.
